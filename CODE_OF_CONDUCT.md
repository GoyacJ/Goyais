# Code of Conduct

[简体中文](CODE_OF_CONDUCT.zh-CN.md) | English

## Our Standard

We are committed to a respectful, inclusive, and harassment-free community.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Focus on technical issues, not individuals.
- Provide clear evidence for claims and critiques.
- Assume good intent and collaborate in good faith.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, hate speech, or personal attacks
- Threats, intimidation, or doxxing
- Deliberate disruption or bad-faith discussion

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, remove, or reject content and contributions that violate this policy.

Repeated or severe violations may lead to temporary or permanent participation restrictions.
